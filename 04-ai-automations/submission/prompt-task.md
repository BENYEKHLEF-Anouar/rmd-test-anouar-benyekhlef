# Part A — Prompt task

## AI tool used
Claude, Sonnet 4.6, free tier

## Why I picked this tool
Claude follows tightly constrained copy briefs well, including character limits and brand voice rules, without needing much correction.

## Prompt(s) I used

> You are helping with SEO copy for a fictional coffee brand called Atlas Coffee Tangier.
>
> The brand voice is warm, grounded, and confident — a small coffee shop that takes its beans seriously without being pretentious. Do not use words like "premium", "luxury", "world-class", or "unforgettable". Do not overclaim.
>
> The brand focuses on single-origin beans sourced from family farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Yemen. They roast twice a week in small batches under 8 kg. Coffee is brewed by hand — pour-over, espresso, and Moroccan-style filter. They are located in the medina of Tangier and have been open since 2019.
>
> I need 3 different H1 + meta description variations for a new product page for the brand's signature blend.
>
> Constraints:
> - Target keyword: "specialty coffee tangier"
> - Each H1 should read like a real product page H1 — short, declarative, not stuffed.
> - Each H1 should be around 50–65 characters if possible.
> - Each meta description must be 150–160 characters maximum.
> - The keyword should appear naturally in at least the H1 of each variation, ideally also in the meta description.
> - Use three different angles: provenance (where the beans come from), craft (how it is roasted), and location (the medina, Tangier).
> - Use concrete details from the brand — farm sourcing, roast frequency, place names — not generic marketing language.
> - Output only the 3 variations in a numbered list.
> - For each variation, include:
>   - H1
>   - Meta description

## Raw output from the AI (all 3 variations, unedited)

1. **H1:** `Specialty Coffee Tangier, Beans We Trace to the Farm` (52 chars)
   **Meta:** `Atlas Coffee Tangier works directly with family farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Yemen. Single-origin specialty coffee, slow-roasted in small batches.` (150 chars)

2. **H1:** `Specialty Coffee Tangier, Roasted Twice a Week in Small Batches` (63 chars)
   **Meta:** `At Atlas Coffee Tangier, we roast under 8 kg at a time. The bag you take home was roasted within the last five days. Specialty coffee, done right.` (146 chars)

3. **H1:** `Specialty Coffee Tangier, Brewed by Hand in the Medina` (54 chars)
   **Meta:** `Atlas Coffee Tangier has been serving single-origin specialty coffee in the medina since 2019. Find us just past Bab el Fahs, open Tuesday to Sunday.` (149 chars)

## Final pick + 2-line justification

**Picked variation: #3**

It uses real, specific details — the medina, Bab el Fahs, 2019 — which builds trust without overclaiming, and fits the brand's grounded voice better than the other two.
It also works as a product page H1 that doubles as local SEO copy, which is useful for a physical shop trying to attract foot traffic alongside online readers.
